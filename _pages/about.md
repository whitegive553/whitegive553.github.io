I am an undergraduate student majoring in Computer Science and Statistics at the University of Toronto. I have a strong interest in cutting-edge AI technologies and enjoy applying AI-driven methods to improve efficiency and intelligence in real-world industrial systems. My experience spans both academic research and industry-oriented AI system development, with a focus on building practical, scalable solutions.

## Research Interests
- **Graph Neural Networks (GNN)**: Cross-domain generalization, heterogeneous graph learning
- **Large Language Models (LLM)**: LLM-enhanced systems, semantic alignment
- **AI Applications**: Social network analysis, computer vision, intelligent agents

## Current Focus
I am currently working on LLM-enhanced cross-domain graph neural networks, exploring how large language models can provide semantic priors to enable zero-shot generalization in heterogeneous graph structures. This work addresses the challenge of transferring GNN models across different domains without labeled data.
